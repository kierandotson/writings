---
title: This isn't the kind of question
date: 2025-03-14
tags: [poem]
---

This isn't the kind of question

If I stack n books x inches high lay down a plywood board and push the Hot Wheel and the wind is NNW at 22.5 mph

This can be answered

It's what do I do
While the TV glows with pulpy sci-fi
And I lie on the couch with the dogs
Suddenly remembering
That one day I will die

What if
I want to sail a sailboat
Walk thousands of miles through unnamed mountains
Start a diner on the moon

I will lace up my shoes
Pack a getaway bag
Check the clock – 11:31pm
Scribble a note
And lock the back door behind me
