---
title: Epistaxis
date: 2025-03-10
tags: [poem]
---

Let me out of my fucking body

I will love you through the window
Rays of sun and wind

I never could tell you I was sorry
But I know you will find peace
Where I couldn't
In nosebleeds and stomach aches
Deep red bouquets and innocence

I hope that you remember me
With salty hair on the beach

And know I always did my best
To take care of you

And it's ok to be angry –
I always was a selfish bastard

Who never wanted to feel anything at all
I never asked to feel anything at all
